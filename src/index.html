💡 填写说明：多个参数用&连接，s5/http/https/turn/nat64/ip均支持逗号分隔多个地址以实现并发连接取最快。
参数类型功能说明配置示例
s5/socks/gs5/s5allSOCKS5 代理 (直连失败/全局)s5=user1:pass1@host1:port1,user2:pass2@host2:port2
http/ghttp/httpallHTTP 代理 (直连失败/全局)http=user1:pass1@host1:port1,user2:pass2@host2:port2
https/ghttps/httpsallHTTPS 代理 (直连失败/全局)https=user1:pass1@host1:port1,user2:pass2@host2:port2
turn/gturn/turnallTURN 代理 (直连失败/全局)turn=user1:pass1@host1:port1,user2:pass2@host2:port2
nat64/gnat64NAT64 转换 (直连失败/全局)nat64=[2a01:4f9:c010:3f02:64::]
ip/proxyip访问CF地址失败的代理IPip=1.2.3.4:443,5.6.7.8:443
proxyall全局模式 (无s5,http,nat64参数时为纯直连)proxyall=1
